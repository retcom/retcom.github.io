retrobrite

The following instructions were adapted from retr0bright.com

required materials

  • 1/2 pint (200ml) Hydrogen Peroxide, 30% strength
  • 2 level teaspoons of Xanthan Gum
  • 1 level teaspoon of Glycerine
  • 1/4 teaspoonful of Oxy laundry booster
  • 1 teaspoonful hot (not boiling) water

steps

1. In a very small ceramic or plastic bowl/dish, dissolve the Oxy in the hot water.

2. While the Oxy is dissolving, mix the Hydrogen Peroxide and Xanthan gum in the blender for five seconds.

3. Add the Glycerine to that mixture and blend for another five seconds.

4. Let this mixture sit for five minutes. Blend for another five seconds.

5. Just before you apply the gel, thoroughly stir in by hand, the dissolved Oxy/water mix.
